About DeBiasMe

Our Mission

DeBiasMe was created to address the critical challenge of bias in AI systems and human communication. Our mission is to make bias detection accessible, interactive, and educational.

We believe that by providing tools that help visualize and understand bias, we can foster more inclusive and equitable communication in both human-to-human and human-AI interactions.

Our Team

Our team consists of researchers and developers based in UCL dedicated to improving AI fairness and transparency. Combining expertise in data visualization, software engineering and human-computer interaction, we've developed this tool to bring bias detection into everyday conversations.

Open Source

DeBiasMe is an open-source project. We welcome contributions from developers, researchers, and enthusiasts who want to help improve bias detection and visualization.

Visit our GitHub repository to learn how you can contribute.
